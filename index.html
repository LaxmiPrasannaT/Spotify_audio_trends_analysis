Metrics Glossary

Valence (0.0 - 1.0)

Measures emotional tone. High valence is happy/euphoric; low valence is sad/melancholic.

Energy (0.0 - 1.0)

Represents perceptual intensity, speed, and noisiness (e.g. metal vs. classical suite).

Danceability (0.0 - 1.0)

Suitability for dancing based on tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and regularity.

Loudness (dB)

Average track volume in decibels. Values range between -60dB (very quiet) and 0dB.
